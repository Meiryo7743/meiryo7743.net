Contact form

If you want to get in touch with me, visit Google Form. I will respond you in a few days, but it may need more time to do so depending on your inquiry.

In the contact form, you must fill out the following information, which are used for the purpose of replying. I do not disclose the content to any third party without your concent, except as required by law.

  • Language: Responds are written in the language you chose.
  • Name: Name: If the inquiry was sent from a real person or organization, this information may be used in order to prevent identity theft.
  • Email address: Reply to this email address. Do not use disposable email addresses, and make sure you have granted permission to receive emails from @gmail.com.
  • Subject: Input the summary of your inquiry.
  • Body: Write the details of your inquiry here.
